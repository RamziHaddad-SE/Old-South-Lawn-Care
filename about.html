Who We Are

Dependable Lawn Care, Right at Home

Old South Lawn Care serves homeowners and businesses throughout Northport, Alabama with practical, dependable lawn maintenance and property care.

Our focus is simple: keep your grass healthy, your edges clean, and your property looking its best, week after week. From routine mowing and trimming to weed control, fertilization support, and seasonal cleanups, we handle the regular work so you don't have to think about it.

We're not a giant franchise or a luxury landscape design firm — we're the dependable local crew you call when you want your lawn handled properly, consistently, and without the stress.

We Understand Local Lawns & Seasons

Alabama lawns have their own rhythm — fast warm-season growth, hard-pushing weeds, sudden storms, and heavy fall leaves.

Because we work in this area every week, we understand seasonal growth, summer heat, heavy rain, and the steady maintenance local properties need to stay clean and healthy. That local knowledge is exactly why so many homeowners and businesses keep us on a recurring schedule.
